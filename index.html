Who We Are

Founded in 2012, we set out to provide independent natural living and beauty retailers with access to brands and products that lack distribution in their territory. When importing and exporting, independent retailers are at a major disadvantage. Lacking the resources to navigate the complex regulatory process, import/export paperwork, labeling requirements, and other bureaucratic hurdles, independent retailers were shut out of international market. No longer.

A Sampling Of Our Services Include:

  • Arranging all international freight and due to our large volume of shipments, pass along our substantial savings to our customers.

  • Arranging all import/export permits and documentation.

  • Verifying that all labeling is in compliance with local laws and regulations. In the case where product labeling is not in compliance, we are able to work with our network of attorneys to produce labeling in the local language that is in compliance with all local laws and regulations.

  • Verify product ingredients are not restricted in the destination country.

  • Get access to our network of independent cosmetic and natural health retailers around the world!
